A Breif History of Rickrolling

Rickrolling is one the most enduring memes in history of the internet. Typically rickrolling involves using a disguised hyperlink to the video for Rick Astlty's 1987 single "Never Gonna Give You Up", but has taken many other forms, including suprise preformances from Rick himself.
